dXpln coordinate transform derivative wrt theta for planar vectors.

dXpln(theta,r) calculates the derivative of the coordinate transform matrix from A to B coordinates for planar motion vectors, where coordinate frame B is located at point r (2D vector expressed in A coords) relative to frame A, and is rotated by an angle theta (radians) relative to A.
